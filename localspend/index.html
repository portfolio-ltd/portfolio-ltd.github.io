Features

Automatic tracking.
Zero effort.

All the power of a finance app — none of the privacy trade-offs. No manual entry. No syncing. Just your data, sorted.

📲
SMS Auto-Import
17 regex patterns parse BHIM UPI, Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, WhatsApp Pay, HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank, Visa, Mastercard, Amex — and more.
🏷️
Smart Auto-Categories
120+ merchant keywords auto-assign transactions to Food, Travel, Shopping, Utilities, Health, Fuel, Rent, EMI, Entertainment, and UPI P2P. Your corrections apply retroactively.
📊
Instant Dashboard
Summary cards for Today, This Week, and This Month front and centre. Recent transactions feed below, with a FAB to the full list.
🔍
Full-Text Search
FTS5-powered full-text search finds any transaction instantly. Filter by category, sort by date or amount — with a smooth animated filter panel.
🗄️
Local SQLite Database
All data lives in a WAL-mode SQLite database on your device, managed via Drift ORM with codegen-backed type-safe queries. Fast, reliable, yours.
🔐
Biometric Lock
Protect your financial data with Face ID or Fingerprint authentication. App won't open without you — no passcode fallback required.
🚫
Zero Telemetry
Not a single network call is made from the data layer. No analytics SDK. No crash reporter phoning home. What's on your phone stays on your phone.
↩️
Swipe to Delete
Remove any transaction with a quick swipe gesture. Clean, fast, and satisfying — your ledger, your rules.

How it works

Set up in seconds.
Runs forever.

1
Grant SMS permission
LocalSpend requests read-only SMS access once. It only reads payment-related messages — never personal conversations.
2
Transactions appear automatically
Your inbox is scanned on-device. Every debit gets parsed and stored locally in seconds — no manual entry, ever.
3
Smart categories, instantly
Transactions land in the right bucket automatically. Fix a category once and it applies retroactively to all matching merchants.
4
Search and review anytime
Full-text search your entire ledger, filter by category, sort how you like, and swipe to delete anything you don't need.
