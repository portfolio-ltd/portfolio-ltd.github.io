Sky Voyage

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16 April 2026 · Last Updated: 16 April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sky Voyage collects, uses, and protects information when you download and use the app from the Google Play Store.

Sky Voyage is an arcade hot air balloon game developed by Sky Voyage Studio. The app is currently available in closed release testing on Google Play.

2. Information we collect

Sky Voyage is built to collect as little data as possible. The app does not require registration, and no personal account is created inside the game.

  • No name, email address, or phone number is collected by the app.
  • No analytics or behavioural tracking is collected by Sky Voyage itself.
  • Google Play Games sign-in is used only for achievements, leaderboards, and cloud save support when you choose to sign in. Any data sent through Google Play Games is governed by Google Play's privacy policies.

3. In-app purchases

Sky Voyage offers optional in-app purchases for cosmetic currency and premium items. All purchases are processed via Google Play.

  • Gem packs (cosmetic premium currency): $0.99 – $19.99
  • The app does not require payment to progress or access gameplay content.

4. Third-party services

Sky Voyage uses Google Play services for distribution, payments, and optional cloud save features. The app may also use standard system permissions required for gameplay and notifications.

We do not share or sell your personal information with third parties.

5. Children's privacy

This app is rated Everyone (E) and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the new version will be posted here with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@sagarchandagarwal.com. Developer: Sagar Chand Agarwal.
