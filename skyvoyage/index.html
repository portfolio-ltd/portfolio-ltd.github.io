Full description

🎈 SKY VOYAGE — THE ULTIMATE HOT AIR BALLOON ADVENTURE

Take to the skies in Sky Voyage, the exhilarating endless balloon game where skill, timing, and nerves of steel are your only fuel!

CONTROL YOUR BALLOON
Hold and drag to steer your hot air balloon through a living, breathing sky. Fire the burner to climb, release to drift — every movement matters. Enable gyroscope steering and physically tilt your phone to bank and soar.

6 BREATHTAKING BIOMES

  • Peaceful Meadows (0–2 km) — Rolling hills, gentle breezes, and golden coins
  • Mountain Passes (2–5 km) — Jagged peaks, thorn brambles, and narrow corridors
  • Storm Clouds (5–10 km) — Lightning bolts, hail bursts, and charged spike clouds
  • Desert Dunes (10–18 km) — Scorching thermals, sandstorms, and sky pirates
  • Arctic Tundra (18–30 km) — Blizzards, frozen spike formations, and rival balloons
  • Sky Kingdom (30+ km) — Endless skies, legendary obstacles, and ultimate glory

COLLECT & POWER UP
Grab coins to unlock spectacular balloon skins. Speed Boost, Shield Bubble, Coin Magnet, Slow Time, and more! Earn rare gems to unlock premium cosmetics. Watch your fuel gauge — grab Fuel Tanks before you fall!

CUSTOMISE YOUR BALLOON
Deck out your balloon in the Hangar with unique envelope skins, basket styles, crew members, and flags. Every piece gives stat bonuses — choose wisely!

CLIMB THE LEADERBOARDS
Compete against players worldwide on the global distance leaderboard, or go head-to-head with friends. Unlock 30+ achievements and prove you're the greatest aviator in the skies.

FEATURES

  • Smooth drag & gyroscope controls — steer with touch or tilt
  • Infinite procedurally generated world with seamless biome transitions
  • Full progression system: XP, levels (1–50+), prestige ranks
  • Three upgrade trees: Balloon Tech, Navigation Skills, Crew Training
  • Zone-exclusive obstacles — every biome has its own unique hazards
  • Rewarded video ads: revive your run, double your coins, grab a free power-up
  • Beautiful parallax sky environments with day/night cycle
  • Immersive zone-specific music for every biome
  • Haptic feedback and satisfying SFX
  • Google Play Games: cloud saves, achievements, leaderboards
  • Dark mode support and adaptive icon
