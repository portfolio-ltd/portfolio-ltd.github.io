Welcome to Saransh ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). Saransh is a personal wealth and family records management application that helps you organise financial documents, family member details, insurance policies, investments, property and legal records in one secure place.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect and share information when you use the Saransh mobile application and associated services. By using Saransh you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

We are based in India. This policy is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information — device model, OS version, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data — screens visited, features used, session duration (no keystroke or field-level logging)
  • Crash logs — stack traces, error codes to help us fix bugs
  • Network data — IP address, request timestamps (for fraud prevention)

1.3 Biometric Authentication Data

If you enable biometric login (fingerprint / face), the biometric template is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave (Android BiometricPrompt API). We never receive or store biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account, authenticate your identity
  • Provide the core features of the App — dashboard, vault, alerts, family records
  • Send expiry and renewal alerts for documents, insurance and loans
  • Improve application performance, fix bugs and develop new features
  • Prevent fraud, abuse and unauthorised access
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to your support requests
We do not use your financial or personal records for advertising, profiling or sale to third parties.

Legal Bases (DPDPA 2023)

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the App service you signed up for
  • Consent — optional features such as biometric login or marketing communications
  • Legitimate interest — security monitoring, fraud prevention, product analytics
  • Legal obligation — compliance with applicable Indian law

Data Storage & Security

Encryption

  • At rest — all sensitive fields are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage in our database
  • In transit — all communication between the App and our servers uses TLS 1.3
  • On device — locally cached credentials are stored in Android EncryptedSharedPreferences (Keystore-backed AES-256)

Infrastructure

Our backend API (api.saransh.app) is hosted on secured cloud infrastructure with restricted access controls, automated security patches, and regular vulnerability assessments.

Access Controls

  • Only authorised personnel have access to production data
  • All staff access is logged and audited
  • We use multi-factor authentication on all internal systems
No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use industry-standard measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong unique password and enable biometric authentication.

Data Location

Your data is stored on servers located in India. We do not transfer personal data outside India unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the App. Upon deletion, all your personal data will be permanently erased within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

Push Notifications

You can disable expiry alerts and push notifications via your device's system settings or from Profile → Settings → Notifications in the App.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. Specific retention periods:

  • Account data — retained for the lifetime of your account, plus 30 days after deletion
  • Financial records — deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Server access logs — retained for up to 90 days for security purposes
  • Crash reports — retained for up to 12 months in anonymised form
  • Legal obligations — where law requires longer retention (e.g. tax, audit), we retain only the minimum necessary data

Children's Privacy

Saransh is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Family member records may include names and relations of minor family members as entered by an adult account holder.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a person under 18 without verified parental consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete the data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an in-app notification and/or email to registered users
  • For significant changes, request fresh consent where required by law

Continued use of the App after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
