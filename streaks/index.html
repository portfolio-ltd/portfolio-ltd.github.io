Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built around behavioral science, Streaks keeps things simple so you actually use it every day.

🔥
Streak Tracking
Visual flame streaks that grow with your consistency. Current streak, longest streak, and total completions — all at a glance.
😊
Mood Logging
Rate how you feel on a 5-point scale with every check-in. Spot patterns between your habits and your mood over time.
🔔
Smart Reminders
Per-habit daily notifications at your chosen time. Optional early heads-up 15 or 30 minutes before, and a streak-risk alert in the evening.
📊
Progress Charts
7-day completion bar charts, per-habit heatmaps, and an advanced metrics view with day-of-week breakdowns and completion rates.
🏅
Badge System
Earn tiered emoji badges as your streak grows — from Seedling 🌱 all the way to Diamond 💎. Unlock them, keep them forever.
🗓️
Flexible Schedules
Set habits for every day, specific weekdays, or a weekly target of 1–7 days. Not all habits need to be daily.
🔒
100% Private
All your data lives on your device. No login, no cloud sync, no tracking. Export and import a local JSON backup whenever you like.
✨
Beautiful Design
Liquid Glass interface on iOS with a frosted gradient backdrop. Full Material 3 dark mode on Android. Fast, focused, distraction-free.

Review your progress in the Stats and Advanced Metrics screens. See which days you're strongest and where to improve.
